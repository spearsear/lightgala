LightGala Startup

    LightGala is a new software service over the web developed by Optimow Inc. Optimow Inc provides an easy-to-use mobile estimator to contractors to help them quote the jobs very efficiently. Many contractors doing Christmas decoration during winter time use this mobile estimator yet they require a more fancy online decoration tool to demo to their potential customers the fancy "real effect" which plain words or descriptions would not deliver.

    Hence the birth of LightGala. It is a platform for home owners or decoration professionals to decorate the house (or any site) online, they can then communicate through LightGala to exchange the decorated effect and proudly publish it to the general public or among their friends.

    For decoration professionals, LightGala is their most important marketing channel. As millions of homeowners are visiting LightGala for lighting decoration ideas, they are the potential customers of decoration professionals. LightGala also makes the estimating work very easy, it could count number of bulbs of each kind, measure distances, give an estimated dollar amount, and integrate with your quoting system like Optimow Mobile Estimator. Send an inquiry to spearsear@gmail.com for pricing if you are a decoration pro.

    For homeowners, LightGala is a mini decoration game on line. At absolutely no cost, any homeowner can light up his or her house in virtual reality and share it among the friends and families, or even proudly publish to the world.

    For lighting equipment suppliers, LightGala is the perfect choice for vendoring the fancy lighting gadgets to homeowners or decoration pros. By becoming authorized supplier of LightGala, your lighting gadgets will be listed in the LightGala widgets so everyone can try decorating their house (or site) using your gadgets.

    To become an authorized supplier of LightGala, send an inquiry to spearsear@gmail.com
